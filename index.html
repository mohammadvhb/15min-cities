What Are 15-Minute Cities?

"15-Minute Cities" is a concept in sustainable urban planning that focuses on creating cities and neighborhoods where people can meet their daily needs within a 15-minute walk or bike ride. In 15-Minute Cities, residential areas, services, and essential facilities are located close to each other. This idea is recognized as a study model worldwide and offers benefits such as reduced traffic congestion and improved quality of life.
